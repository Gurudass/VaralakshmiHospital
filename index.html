Welcome to New Varalakshmi Hospital - Your Trusted Healthcare Partner

New Varalakshmi Hospital is a NABH Pre-accredited healthcare facility dedicated to providing comprehensive medical services with a focus on patient care and clinical excellence.

Welcome to New Varalakshmi Hospital, a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing exceptional medical services with a compassionate touch and a commitment to innovation. Our hospital, NABH Pre-accredited, is a trusted name in healthcare, offering a comprehensive range of specialties including General Medicine, Orthopedics, Neurosurgery, Oncology, and Urology.

Why Choose Our Hospital !

Caring For The Health And Well Being Of You And Your Family.

Experience advanced medical care, personalized treatment plans, state-of-the-art facilities, and compassionate staff dedicated to your well-being.

When you choose New Varalakshmi Hospital, you are choosing excellence in healthcare. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with advanced technology to support accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. With 10 ICU beds and 5 ventilators, we are prepared to provide critical care when you need it most. Our team of expert doctors is renowned for their expertise and dedication to patient care, ensuring that you receive personalized treatment plans tailored to your individual needs.

With modern, busy lifestyles, it’s easy to neglect your health as you put everyone else’s needs first. So, we’re here to care for you and your entire family.
